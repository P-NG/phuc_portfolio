Overview

Designing an experience from the ground up is both challenging and rewarding, requiring thoughtful decisions and close collaboration. Our efforts are driven by a shared mission: to create a journaling experience that helps people reflect on daily moments, fostering greater presence with themselves and their environment.

The Problem & Objectives

We believe journaling should go beyond recording daily events-- It's a time for thoughtful reflection. Yet, no tools truly support this. That's why we're creating a journaling solution designed to help people reflect on their thoughts in a more organized way. Our primary objectives include:

  • Create an intuitive navigation system that lets users effortlessly trace conversations in their timeline for easy reflection.
  • Design tools that reveal patterns to help users visualize their progress.

My Contribution

Create a tool that helps people reflect on their past in an organized way, offering new perspectives and allowing them to track their personal growth over time. This involved doing user interviews, drafting plans, documenting findings, and creating prototypes at various fidelities for internal testing and development.

The Rabbit Hole

Ideate

I created high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes for the app's core "rabbit hole" feature, which used a breadcrumb system to let users retrace their journaling journey from any point-- child-reflections, parent-reflections, or the original entry. The goal was to help users spot patterns and track growth. However, due to limited resources and data, we simplified the system by removing the ability to reflect on reflections. We plan to revisit this feature when more resources are available.

Archived Rabbit Hole

  • Sheet scroll through timeline from latest to oldest entries
  • Sheet navigate to Parent entry (full page) - quick access to Original entry (full page)
  • Preview card on full page (Parent/Original) open sheet
  • Close sheet and return to full page before activating sheet

MVP Rabbit Hole

  • Preview card open full page
  • Reflection can onlly be made to a single Original entry
  • No reflection of a reflection
  • Access all reflections by tapping Preview mode of the Original Entry
