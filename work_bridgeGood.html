PROBLEMS

Developing an original and modern UI that is functional and represent Bridgegood as a organization.

Redirecting visitors of oaklanddigtal.org to bridgegood.com effortlessly and informing the visitors about the rebrand

RESUME PAGE OVERVIEW

My team and I were assigned to redesign the artist's about page to a resume page for BRIDGEGOOD's portfolio platform. From our user research, we've concluded it is helpful for a creator to have the ability to display their resume alongside their portfolio so employers can get a complete overview of the creative with quickly and efficiently.

TEAM

Devon Washington, Tinh Nguyen, Jamaica Scott, and Shaun Tai

ROLE

Facilitating brainstorm sessions • Prototyping web layout • Communicating with Product Lead • Oraganizing deliverables and delivering all final prototyes for development

PROBLEMS

Converting the “artist about” page to a “resume” page that display expected information in a conventional resume while retaining elements from the previous “artist about” and portfolio page.

GOALS

Design a secondary webpage from the portfolio page that present the creative as a professional to employers.

UX CHALLENGES

Displaying both conventional information in a resume and unique questions pertain to creatives on Bridgegood platform. Also, designing an intuitive editing process of the resume page.

SOLUTIONS

Dividing the resume and Q&A’s into two sections within the page to showcase the creative’s professional experience and personal characteristics. As well of incorporating secondary stastics unique to the platform that were remove from the portfolio page.

UNDERSTANDING

From our qualitative research, we’ve concluded that early career creatives find it helpful to have the ability to showcase their resume along side their portfolio. Creatives like the idea of having both major components for creative role within the webpage so employers can easily access both components which in turn eliminating the pain point for employers to switch between portfolio and the pdf of the resume.
